## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [https://darkswap.io](https://darkswap.io) and click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.
2. Enter your email address and create a strong password.
3. Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to your inbox.
4. Complete your profile by providing the required information.
5. Set up two-factor authentication (2FA) for additional security.

### Setting Up Your Wallet

DarkSwap supports various wallet types:

#### Built-in Wallet

1. Go to the "Wallet" section in the main navigation.
2. Click on "Create New Wallet".
3. Follow the instructions to create a new wallet.
4. **Important**: Write down your recovery phrase and store it in a safe place. This is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access.

#### External Wallet

DarkSwap supports connecting to external wallets:

1. Go to the "Wallet" section in the main navigation.
2. Click on "Connect Wallet".
3. Select your wallet provider from the list (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect).
4. Follow the instructions to connect your wallet.

### Navigating the Interface

The DarkSwap interface consists of several main sections:

#### Header Navigation

- **Home**: Returns to the main dashboard.
- **Trade**: Access the trading interface.
- **Orders**: View and manage your orders.
- **Wallet**: Access your wallet and transaction history.
- **Settings**: Configure your account and preferences.

#### Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of:

- Market statistics
- Your recent trades
- Your open orders
- Your wallet balances
- Important announcements

#### Trading Interface

The trading interface includes:

- Order book: Shows current buy and sell orders.
- Price chart: Displays historical price data.
- Order form: Used to create buy and sell orders.
- Trade history: Shows recent trades for the selected pair.

#### Wallet Interface

The wallet interface includes:

- Balance overview: Shows your current balances.
- Deposit: Generate addresses for depositing funds.
- Withdraw: Send funds to external addresses.
- Transaction history: View your past transactions.

#### Settings

The settings page allows you to:

- Update your profile information.
- Change your password.
- Configure two-factor authentication.
- Set notification preferences.
- Customize the interface.
